About Me

Hello, I'm Charles. Some of my friends call me Chuck. I am 26 years old from Kitty Hawk, NC. I like to play guitar and piano, learn all kinds of random stuff, exercise, listen to music, read, and hang out with friends. My favorite things are surfing and boating, or really just anything involving water.

I have always been interested in technology, but I originally wanted to be a dentist until a year or so ago when I decided to at least postpone that dream a few years. I taught myself some Salesforce skills and got certified as a Salesforce Platform Administrator and now work for a company called Validity that makes software tools to improve the data quality of databases (mostly Salesforce databases). Check out one of my tutorial videos I made for one of our products here.


Over the past four years, I've worked as a construction worker, a bicycle technician, hail damage repair technician, photobooth manager, and customer success manager for a software company. Now I am excited to learn web-development and be solving fun problems again!
